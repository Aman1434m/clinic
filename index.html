Connect Your n8n Workflow
Import your n8n workflow to use as an AI agent. Paste the webhook URL from your n8n workflow trigger node.
How to Setup
Step 1: In n8n, create a workflow with a Webhook trigger node.
Step 2: Add AI processing nodes (OpenAI, Claude, etc.) after the webhook.
Step 3: End with a Respond to Webhook node that returns JSON: { "response": "..." }
Step 4: Paste webhook URL above & connect. Your n8n workflow becomes an AI agent here!
